# Public Roadmap

This roadmap is intentionally high-level. It describes the public direction of FlowAxion Business Reality Engine without exposing private implementation details.

## Phase 1 — Public manifesto

Status: in progress

Goals:

- publish the public concept;
- define the core terminology;
- document ownership and copyright;
- establish the public/private boundary;
- present the FlowAxion vision clearly.

Public outputs:

- README;
- concept paper;
- vision document;
- glossary;
- public roadmap;
- copyright and notice files.

## Phase 2 — Field Scan prototype

Goal:

Allow a business owner to record a short video of the company environment and receive an initial structured list of detected assets, areas, documents, and operational elements.

Expected outputs:

- video upload or capture;
- frame extraction;
- audio transcription;
- object and context detection;
- initial business memory draft;
- owner confirmation flow.

## Phase 3 — Business Memory foundation

Goal:

Create a structured memory layer for company identity, assets, documents, people, services, workflows, market context, and missing information.

Expected outputs:

- memory schema;
- confidence states;
- confirmation workflow;
- document association;
- entity relationships;
- audit trail.

## Phase 4 — Reality-to-Action workflows

Goal:

Use Business Memory to generate useful operational outputs.

Examples:

- inventory summaries;
- missing-document lists;
- grant-readiness checks;
- service catalogs;
- website drafts;
- collaborator task lists;
- maintenance reminders;
- market positioning suggestions.

## Phase 5 — Co-design Studio

Goal:

Allow business owners, collaborators, and AI to co-design outputs and workflows together.

Examples:

- website structure;
- offers and service pages;
- operational processes;
- company documentation;
- marketing campaigns;
- internal workflows.

## Phase 6 — Agentic operations

Goal:

Enable AI agents to act with grounded company context while respecting permissions, validation, human approval, and operational safety.

Expected areas:

- administrative assistant;
- commercial assistant;
- inventory assistant;
- grant assistant;
- marketing assistant;
- workflow assistant.

## Non-goals for this public repository

This repository does not publish:

- source code;
- private architecture;
- internal prompts;
- production schemas;
- customer data;
- commercial strategy;
- detailed implementation plans.
